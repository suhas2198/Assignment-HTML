Association football , more commonly known as simply football or soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players who primarily use their feet to propel the ball around a rectangular field called a pitch. the objective of the game is to score more Goals than opposition by moving the ball beyond the goal line into opposing side's rectangular framed goal.traditonally the game is played over two 45 minute halves for total match time is 90 minutes with an estimated 250 players active in over 200 ountries ,Its is considerd as worlds most popular sport
